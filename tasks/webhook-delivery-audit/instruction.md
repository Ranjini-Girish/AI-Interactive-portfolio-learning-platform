Our outbound webhook platform dumped per-endpoint delivery logs under `/app/deliveries/` and endpoint registry entries under `/app/endpoints/`. Policy thresholds and severity mapping live in `/app/config/policy.json`; named retry schedules are in `/app/config/retry_policies.json`. A Node starter is at `/app/src/main.js`.

Implement the auditor in TypeScript only. Run it with `TypeScript on Node 22 /app/src/main.js` after you finish. It must write `/app/output/webhook_audit.json` and `/app/output/checksum.sha256` (SHA-256 of the exact JSON bytes, two spaces, then `webhook_audit.json`). Use 2-space JSON indentation and a trailing newline on the report. Do not modify anything under `/app/config/`, `/app/endpoints/`, or `/app/deliveries/`.

For each endpoint, validate delivery attempt signatures, replay whether retries respect the configured backoff, and emit findings when policy rules are violated. Per-endpoint metrics and global summary fields must follow `/app/docs/output_schema.md`. Signature canonicalization, retry timing math, finding types, risk scoring, and sort orders are defined in `/app/docs/signing_rules.md`, `/app/docs/retry_rules.md`, and `/app/docs/findings_catalog.md`.
